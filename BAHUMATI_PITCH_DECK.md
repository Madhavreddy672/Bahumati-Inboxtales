### Traditional Gifting Is Broken

- ❌ **Gifts lose value immediately** — sweets, clothes, gadgets depreciate to zero
- ❌ **No long-term impact** — receiver gains nothing after the moment passes
- ❌ **Zero compounding** — money spent never works again

### The India-Specific Gap

- 🇮🇳 Indians are **habitual gifters** — culturally, ritually, and socially

- 🚫 **No bridge exists** between these two deeply embedded behaviours

### The Cost of This Gap
- Trillions of rupees gifted annually — generating **zero financial return**
- Every missed gifting occasion is a **lost compounding event**
