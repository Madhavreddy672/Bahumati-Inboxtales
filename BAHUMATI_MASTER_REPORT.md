## 6. PRODUCTS & SERVICES — DEEP TECHNICAL BREAKDOWN

### 6.1 Core Product
> **Bahumati Gift → Digi Units → Regulated Investment → Growth Tracking**

### 6.2 How Bahumati Works — Step-by-Step (Corrected Model)

**Step 1️⃣ — Gifter Initiates a Gift**
- Downloads the Bahumati app & completes **KYC**
- Enters receiver's mobile number
- Chooses gift amount (e.g. ₹100, ₹500, ₹1,000…)
- Pays — **gift is created instantly**

**Step 2️⃣ — Money Handling (Escrow)**
- ₹100 goes into a **temporary escrow account**
- Money is **NOT yet invested** — receiver's KYC is not done
- Funds held safely until receiver claims

**Step 3️⃣ — Receiver Gets Notified & Claims**
- Receiver gets notification reminders for up to **90 days**
- If receiver **downloads app + completes KYC**:
  - ₹100 gets **invested in receiver's name**
  - Mutual fund folio created
  - Units allotted to receiver's account
  - Receiver can: let it grow 📈 or redeem to bank later 💰

**Step 4️⃣ — If Receiver Does NOT Register in 90 Days**
- Gift **expires automatically**
- ₹100 is **fully refunded** back to the gifter
- No money is lost by either party

**Step 5️⃣ — Optional Emotional Layer — Love Memo**
A digital emotional add-on attached to the gift:
- Photo 📸
- Written message 📝
- Voice note 🎙️
- 1-minute video 🎥

> **Emotion + Investment = High Retention**

**Investment Instruments (MVP Phase):**
- **UTI Nifty 50 Index Fund (Direct – Growth)**
- **Sovereign Gold Bonds / Gold FoF**

Why these instruments?
- Simple & Transparent
- Low risk, long-term compounding friendly
- Regulator-approved
